# Example: Protocol Holders Direct Vs With DeFi

Prompt:

> Should I use protocol_token_holders or protocol_token_holders_with_defi?

Flow:

1. Use `etherfi-catalog` MCP to compare or inspect both datasets.
2. Explain that the direct-holders view is the cleaner direct wallet holder view.
3. Explain that the with-DeFi view includes tracked DeFi exposure but may be incomplete.
4. Ask whether indirect DeFi exposure should be included.
5. Ask whether rows identified as DeFi contracts should be excluded.

Do not silently substitute one holder dataset for the other.
